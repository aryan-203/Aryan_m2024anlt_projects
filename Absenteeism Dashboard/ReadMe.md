## Introduction
This Power BI dashboard delivers a comprehensive overview of employee absenteeism within a retail organization, leveraging detailed attendance and HR data to visualize trends, risks, and workforce costs[file:2]. The goal is to empower HR and operations teams with actionable, real-time insights.

## Data Origin
The underlying dataset includes records for 3499 employees, tracking demographics, job information, department, division, shift, joining date, absence details (date, type, reason, status), cost metrics (transportation expense, absenteeism cost), and disciplinary actions. Data covers periods in 2023 and 2024[file:2].

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop (visual analytics and dashboards)
- Excel or SQL (for any supplementary data wrangling)

## Dashboard Features

- **Key Metrics:** Total employees, average workload, total absenteeism cost (approx. 7M), and attendance status visualized instantly[file:2].
- **Leave Type Distribution:** Pie chart displays paid, unpaid, and medical leaves for quick breakdown.
- **Department & Division Analysis:** Bar charts show attendance and absenteeism by department and division, spotlighting problematic areas.
- **Shift Pattern Analysis:** Stacked bar chart visualizes morning, evening, and night shift absenteeism.
- **Temporal Trends:** Monthly and seasonal absence tracked with line/bar charts, identifying peak seasons (e.g., winter/spring).
- **Absenteeism vs. Distance:** Histogram connects frequency of absences with commuting distance, highlighting transportation challenges.
- **Planned vs. Unplanned Absence:** Donut chart for split between scheduled and unscheduled absences.
- **Experience Distribution:** Chart showing number of employees by years of service.

## Insights Generated

- High unscheduled absenteeism rates highlight the importance of absence management and intervention[file:2].
- Absenteeism clusters in departments such as meats and customer service call for targeted strategies.
- Seasonal peaks in absenteeism (winter, spring) inform staffing plans and mitigation efforts.
- Financial analysis quantifies absenteeism cost (~7M), critical for HR strategic planning.
- Commute distance correlates with absence frequency, suggesting the need for potential location or policy changes.

## Usage

1. Open the dashboard Power BI (.pbix) file.
2. Use interactive filters to explore absenteeism by year, department, shift, experience, and other dimensions.
3. Drill into visualizations to identify actionable insights for workforce planning or policy decisions.

This dashboard provides HR and management teams with a single point of access to deep absenteeism analytics, enabling data-driven strategies, cost control, and continuous workplace improvement[file:2].
